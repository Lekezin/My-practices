Kamaitachi

Kamaitachi reached the peak of his career with the album Homem Torto, which was composed of songs with peculiar lyrics, mentioning entities such as the one that gave the album its name, and telling stories like in the songs Cabelos Arco-Íris and 6balas, the latter receiving a sequel in 2023 titled 6balas (Act II).
